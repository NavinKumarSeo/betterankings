Honest profiles

Profound — the enterprise standard

The most established answer-engine analytics platform, with deep conversation-level data on how AI assistants discuss your category. Excellent if you're a large brand or agency with the budget and an in-house team to act on the data. The depth is real; so is the price.

AthenaHQ — mid-market analytics + optimization

A fast-growing platform that pairs visibility tracking with optimization guidance, aimed at marketing teams that want more than a raw dashboard. A strong middle option between entry-level monitors and enterprise suites.

Otterly.ai — accessible monitoring

One of the more affordable, SMB- and agency-friendly options for tracking whether (and how) you appear in AI search results. Great for getting started and for agencies managing several smaller brands.

Peec AI & Scrunch AI — team and brand monitoring

Peec AI leans into collaborative, team-friendly analytics (popular with European and agency teams); Scrunch AI focuses on brand and reputation monitoring across engines. Both are solid mid-market monitors with their own flavour.

What are GEO tools?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) tools monitor how your brand appears inside AI-generated answers — whether you're mentioned, how prominently, and which sources the engines cite.
