Perplexity SEO: how to become a source Perplexity cites

Perplexity is different from ChatGPT in one decisive way: it searches the live web for every question and cites its sources, numbered, right in the answer. That makes the goal beautifully concrete — to be in a Perplexity answer, you have to be one of the pages it cites. Here's how that selection works, and how to win the citation.

The mechanics

How Perplexity picks its citations

Because it's live-search-first, Perplexity rewards a specific kind of page:

01

It ranks for the query

Perplexity starts from live search results. If you don't surface for the buyer's question in the first place, you're not a candidate to be cited. Traditional ranking still matters here.

02

It's cleanly citable

Perplexity favours pages that are factual, well-structured, and easy to quote — clear claims, comparisons, and data it can lift confidently into an answer.

03

It's trusted & current

Review aggregators, "best of" comparisons and recently-updated sources get cited disproportionately. Freshness and third-party authority both count.

What actually moves it

Winning the citation slot

Of all the AI engines, Perplexity is the most responsive to deliberate work — because you can see exactly which pages it cites and reverse-engineer your way in:

  • Rank for the buyer queries — your SEO partly transfers; surface for the questions buyers actually ask
  • Write citably — lead with the answer, structure facts and comparisons so they're easy to quote
  • Get onto the cited pages — the comparison and review sources Perplexity keeps pulling for your category
  • Keep it current — refresh the pages that matter; Perplexity leans toward the up-to-date
