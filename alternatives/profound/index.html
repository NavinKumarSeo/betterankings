Looking for a Profound alternative?

Profound is the enterprise standard for AI-search analytics — and it's priced like it. If you came here, you probably want what Profound measures (how ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI and Gemini talk about your brand) without the enterprise contract, the steep learning curve, or the in-house team it assumes you have. Here are the honest alternatives — including the one that isn't a tool at all.

First, the honest part

What Profound is great at

Profound is one of the most capable answer-engine analytics platforms on the market: deep, conversation-level data on how AI assistants surface brands, strong competitor tracking, and dashboards built for scale. If you're a large enterprise or an agency managing many brands with a team to act on the data daily, it genuinely earns its price. We'd recommend it for that.

Most people searching for an alternative aren't that buyer. They're a lean marketing team that wants three things Profound doesn't optimise for: a lower price, less complexity, and someone to actually do the work the data implies.
